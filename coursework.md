# Course Work

This page highlights selected coursework and learning artifacts developed as part of my instructional design studies. Each project reflects my growing ability to analyze learning problems, apply theory, and design effective instructional solutions.

---

## Instructional Media & Learning History

**Project:** Instructional Media Timeline  
**Focus:** Historical analysis of instructional media and technology  

**Description:**  
This project examined major developments in instructional media from the early 20th century to the present, with emphasis on access, adoption, and instructional impact. Special attention was given to how technology availability shaped learning opportunities across time periods.

**Skills & Concepts:**
- Instructional media analysis  
- Learning theory connections  
- Historical context in learning design

## Instructional Design Foundations

**Focus:** Behaviorism, Cognitivism, and Constructivism
